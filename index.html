Fun Fair

The Fun Fairs are a part of the Niagra Islamic School were kids go on Trampolines, play Games, and have fun. This Activity is open for any kid in the neighborhood to go see, but in the school Kindergartens can play on the Fun Fairs.

Reviews For School


School Principal
Great Principal!

The school principal in NIS tries to help all the children at NIS and to keep them up and not leave them behind. The Principal always sees if we are focusing well and is sharp and detailed when hearing about us from the teachers. The School Principal also teaches us, teaches us in an unique way. She let's us have fun and fun is also a way to make us be participating. If a student joins NIS at Grade 5, 4, 6, 7, or 8, the Principal always shows the new student around and doesn't leave the new student lonely or left out. All thanks to the school staff, students, teachers, and School Principal, the first top Islamic School in Ontario curriculum is NIS. Ever since NIS (1995) was made, the School Principal changed. From the beginning to now the School Principals were so charming and so nice. Everyone in NIS misses the old principal(s). Now it might be the time to say bye to this one too. Without all of the school Principals, us students would never get ahead... -Aroush
